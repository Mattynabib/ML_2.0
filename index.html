Welcome! I'm Matt Laurence, a Senior UX Designer, musician, video guy, roustabout and rogue. Come on in, have a look around.

My job has been to make life easier for hapless users and professionals alike since 1990. An utter passion for the geekiest of trends in technology and user experience have certainly not hurt.

When my career as technical writer and instructional video producer did not catch fire, I wound up in publishing, then through the Ad Club of Boston in advertising, then into both traditional and digital marketing, and inevitably in what we now call User Experience Design.

For a decade I contracted doing web and Flash strategy, design, and coding work around the Boston area, in between tours with various rock bands. I finally dove head-first - and full-time - into the UX end of web application development (and very early mobile app development via tablet PCs) at Weymouth Design.

From there I joined One To One Interactive and won several MITX awards as a Sr. Interactive Art Director. Since joining Inflexxion in 2010, I've focused on UX design and improving Inflexxion products, as well as building and heading up the Inflexxion creative media services department. I am currently enrolled in the Bentley MSHFID program to receive a Masters in Human Factors and Information Design, planning my escape by 2014.
